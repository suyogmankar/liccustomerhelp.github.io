LIC's Jeevan Umang (Plan No. 945, UIN No. 512N312V02)



LIC’s Jeevan Umang plan offers a combination of income and protection to your family. This plan provides for annual survival benefits from the end of the premium paying term till maturity and a lump sum payment at the time of maturity or on death of the policyholder during the policy term.



Frequently Asked Questions


What kind of policy is this?

LIC Jeevan umang is an endowment cum whole life money back policy.


How much survival benefit is paid?

LIC offers 8% of policy Sum Assured after the premium payment term completed – till policyholder is alive or till the age of 100.


Policy loan is available?

Yes. You will be eligible to get a loan against jeevan umang policy once it acquires a Surrender Value. The loan amount and interest rate would depend on the prevailing at the time of taking the loan.
